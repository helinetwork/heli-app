KYC
----
Step 1: Choose a clear portrait photo of yourself and upload it to the system.
Step 2: Ensure you are in a well-lit environment to record the video. Note: Do not perform under direct sunlight.
Step 3: When the system displays an oval shape, place your face (excluding hair or other decorative items) within the oval frame securely but comfortably.
Step 4: Once your face is fully within the oval frame, pause and wait for the system to establish a direct connection session.
Step 5: Maintain your position until the system completes the scan and displays the result of your KYC verification.
